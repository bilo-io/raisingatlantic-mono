# Workflow: `clinical-report-generation`

> **Trigger:** Provide raw patient data (vaccines, milestones, growth measurements) to `@pediatric-advisor` to initiate the pipeline.

Multi-agent pipeline that transforms raw patient records into a validated, AI-drafted, professionally formatted clinical report.

---

## Step 1 — Data Injection
**Input:** Raw patient data object containing:
- Vaccinations administered (vaccine name, date, batch number, administered by)
- Developmental milestones recorded (milestone, age at achievement, observed by)
- Growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference, date, measured by)

Confirm data is de-identified at the report level (use initials or patient ID, not full name, for report drafting context).

---

## Step 2 — Clinical Validation (`@pediatric-advisor`)
`@pediatric-advisor` audits the data against:
- **EPI-SA 2024/2025**: Check that all scheduled vaccines are recorded, flag overdue or missing doses.
- **WHO Growth Standards**: Plot measurements against age/sex-appropriate z-score charts, flag any readings outside ±2 SD.
- **RTHB/IMCI Milestones**: Check that key milestones are reached within expected age windows.

Output: A structured `ValidationReport` object:
```json
{
  "immunisationStatus": "complete | incomplete | overdue",
  "missingVaccines": [...],
  "growthFlag": "normal | monitor | refer",
  "milestoneFlags": [...],
  "clinicalNotes": "..."
}
```

---

## Step 3 — AI Summary Generation (`@backend` → Genkit)
`@backend` invokes the Genkit AI module (`gemini-2.0-flash`) with:
- The `ValidationReport` from Step 2
- The child's anonymised health context (age, sex, visit history)

Genkit produces a draft clinical summary paragraph covering:
- Immunisation status narrative
- Growth trend narrative
- Developmental milestone summary
- Any flags requiring clinician follow-up

---

## Step 4 — Report Formatting (`@copywriter`)
`@copywriter` receives the AI-drafted summary and refines it into the **Crèche Admission PDF Report** format:

**Report sections:**
1. **Child Profile** (anonymised): Age, sex, date of report
2. **Immunisation Summary**: Status table + narrative
3. **Growth Summary**: Last 3 measurements + flag status
4. **Developmental Milestones**: Achieved milestones + any outstanding flags
5. **Clinical Recommendation**: Clear, plain-language next steps for the caregiver
6. **Legal Disclaimer**: *"This report is generated for informational purposes and does not constitute a medical diagnosis or clinical opinion."*

**Tone:** Professional for the receiving crèche, accessible for the parent receiving a copy.

---

## Step 5 — Output
- Rendered PDF saved to `reports/<patient-id>/<date>-creche-admission.pdf`
- Metadata logged to the `reports` table in the database (patient ID, generated timestamp, generated by user ID)
- Parent and clinician notified via transactional email (drafted by `@copywriter`)
